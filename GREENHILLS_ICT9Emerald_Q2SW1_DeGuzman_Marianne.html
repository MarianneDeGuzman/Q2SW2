Teacup Poodle One of the tiniest canines is the teacup poodle. These adorable puppies fit almost anyplace and are devoted and loving. They may fit in a variety of settings due to their little size, therefore it doesn't matter if your house or apartment is big or small.
Maltese Maltese are loving toy dogs who weigh under seven pounds and have a long, silky coat. A small body with a fluid, effortless walk moves beneath the all-white cloak. The total image exudes harmony and free-flowing grace. The most jaded sense might be won over by the alluring Maltese face with its wide, dark eyes and black gumdrop nose.
Pomeranian It has been said that the little Pomeranian, a longtime favourite of both royalty and commoners, is the perfect companion. The magnificent coat, happy, foxy face, and vibrant nature of the Pom have made it one of the most well-liked toy breeds in the world.
